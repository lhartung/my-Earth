MyEarth

About

MyEarth is an energy tracking application developed by researchers and students at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

How Does It Work?

Simply navigate to the My Activities page to choose activities and perform them. Each activity saves a given amount of Water or Carbon (see Calculations for a comprehensive list of assumptions and calculations). Your full energy savings are tracked and displayed on the Profile screen.
