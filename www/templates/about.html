MyEarth

About

MyEarth is an energy tracking application developed by researchers and students at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. The actual version of MyEarth was developed by Victor Tavares, John Meurer, Andrew Stevens and Nancy Wong. We would like to give special thanks to Sulabh Agarwal and Kevin Kamal who gave us full access to reuse their software and codebase. Their contributions are greatly appreciated.

How Does It Work?

Simply navigate to the My Activities page to choose activities and perform them. Each activity saves a given amount of Water or Carbon (see Calculations for a comprehensive list of assumptions and calculations). Your full energy savings are tracked and displayed on the Profile screen.
