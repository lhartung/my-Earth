DESCRIPTION OF THE RESEARCH

You are invited to participate in a research study from the Wisconsin Smart Energy Lab supported by the National Science Foundation. This project is intended to provide an easy way for people to save energy in their daily lives via the use of an energy diary smartphone app to help them achieve a more sustainable lifestyle.

Your participation in this project is very important to its success. Since this app is still in its testing stage, I would really appreciate it if you can give us your name and email contact in order for us to reach you (only if necessary) regarding ways that we can improve on the app to make it more user friendly and helpful. I want to assure you that all information related to you will remain strictly confidential.

This study will include anyone who owns a smartphone and is interested in using the application. This research requires that the user downloads the app and giving the researcher his/her consent to monitor their usage of the app.

WHAT WILL MY PARTICIPATION INVOLVE?

If you decide to participate in this research you will be asked to download the app onto your smartphone and giving the researcher your name and email contact so that we can ask for your feedback concerning your usage experience.

ARE THERE ANY RISKS TO ME?

We don't anticipate any risks to you from participation in this study.

ARE THERE ANY BENEFITS TO ME?

We don't expect any direct benefits to you from participation in this study.

HOW WILL MY CONFIDENTIALITY BE PROTECTED?

While there will probably be publications as a result of this study, your name will not be used. Only group characteristics will be published.
